What we design

From concept sketch to finished landscape, we handle every phase of your outdoor transformation.

01

Landscape Design

Full-service design plans tailored to Colorado's climate, your property's unique topography, and your lifestyle — from xeriscape to lush native gardens.

02

Outdoor Living Spaces

Patios, pergolas, fire features, and outdoor kitchens designed to extend your living space into the Colorado outdoors naturally.

03

Grading & Drainage

Expert site analysis and grading plans that work with the land — protecting your foundation and creating beautiful, functional transitions.

04

Plant Selection

Curated plant palettes of Colorado natives and climate-adapted species that thrive with minimal water and care while looking exceptional year-round.

05

Irrigation Planning

Water-smart irrigation design that satisfies HOA requirements and local water restrictions without sacrificing beauty or plant health.

06

Construction Documents

Stamped construction drawings ready for permits and contractor bidding — giving you the documentation needed to bring your design to life.
